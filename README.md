# FriendFinder
Find your next BFF

## How it Works
_Friendfinder_ is an app that pairs people with potential best friends. Simply take a 10 question survey and you will be paired with a person who closely matches your personality!

## Technologies Used

* Express
* Material Kit
* Heroku
* jQuery
